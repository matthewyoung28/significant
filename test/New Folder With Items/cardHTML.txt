prior experience building excel models</li><li>Strong financial statement knowledge understanding top and bottom-line drivers.</li><li>Intermediate to Advanced skills in Excel and PowerPoint.</li><li>Familiarity of consumer products, cost structure and sales metrics (ACV, velocity, # items) a plus.</li><li>Experience with Power BI, SQL, and consumption databases (e.g., Nielsen/IRI) is a plus.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong><u>Leadership Skills Required<br/></u></strong><ul><li>Lead and influence all financial aspects of Brand</li><li>Be a strategic business partner who drives thought leadership.</li><li>Take ownership and responsibility for actions, behaviors and contributions.</li><li>Have strong ability to inspire and motivate self and others.</li><li>Be approachable, listen actively and be open to new ideas.</li><li>Able to see opportunities and take calculated risks.<br/><br/></li></ul>Most importantly, you take yourself lightly and your work seriously. And…bring your best self to work every day!<br/><br/><strong>CULTURE</strong>:<br/><br/>At Clif Bar, the journey has always been more important than the destination. We challenge and celebrate one another, we help to balance each other's' lives, and we value laughter and fun as integral qualities in our workplace.<br/><br/>We value diversity and celebrate our people being their most authentic selves. Employees can opt for a flexible work schedule and are encouraged to participate in volunteer opportunities during the workday. Additionally, our people enjoy a paid sabbatical program and sustainability benefits including a commute incentive program. We also provide innovative benefits and holistic wellness offerings including an onsite fitness center (currently closed), personal trainers, paid workout time, as well as concierge services such as life coaching, acupuncture, chiropractic, massage therapy, and organic produce delivery. For family wellbeing, we offer parental and family leave, an onsite childcare center, and an adoption assistance program to help cover related expenses. In 2010, Kit &amp; Gary implemented an Employee Stock Ownership Plan (ESOP) recognizing employees' roles in helping build our business. We have been named best place to work by Outside and Fortune Magazines, and did we mention every day is bring your dog to work day?
